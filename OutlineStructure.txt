Canonical World Coordinate Convention:
 - Unity World Space, with XZ horizontal and Y vertical
 - 1 Unity unit = 1 meter
Exact Chunk Address Type:
 - (chunkX, chunkZ)
 - No vertical chunking for now
Exact World Space Area that Chunk Coodinate (0, 0) covers:
 - [0, chunkSize) in X, [0, chunkSize) in Z
 - By extension, (x, z) covers: [x * chunkSize, (x + 1) * chunkSize), [z * chunkSize, (z + 1) * chunkSize)
What Formula Maps World Position to Chunk Coordinates:
 - (Floor(worldPositionX/chunkSize), Floor(worldPositionZ/chunkSize)) = Chunk Coordinate
Exact Boundary Rules:
 - A point cannot belong to more than one chunk, due to half-open interval design
 - When objects physically span over more than one chunk, one point is considered in which chunk loads it (answered next)
What Point Determines Chunk Ownership for an Object:
 - Pivot of GameObject
Chunk Size:
 - 128 meters
 - Fixed (cannot be modified in video settings)
